# Privacy Policy for NoAIWatermark

## Introduction

Welcome to NoAIWatermark, the **free online tool** designed to help you remove AI watermarks and logos from images quickly and efficiently. At NoAIWatermark, your privacy is of utmost importance to us, and this privacy policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our services.

## Information Collection and Use

We collect the following types of data to provide you with a better experience while using NoAIWatermark:

1. **Account Information**

   - **What We Collect**: NoAIWatermark is designed to work without requiring user accounts. We do not collect personal information such as names or email addresses unless you voluntarily contact us for support.
   - **Purpose**: To provide customer support when requested.

2. **Usage Details**

   - **What We Collect**: Basic analytics about how you use NoAIWatermark, such as page views and feature usage, but no personal identification information.
   - **Purpose**: To analyze user engagement and improve our services.

3. **Device Information**

   - **What We Collect**: Data regarding the devices you use to access NoAIWatermark, such as device type, operating system, and browser type.
   - **Purpose**: To optimize our services for different devices and ensure compatibility.

4. **Cookies**

   - **What We Collect**: Small data files placed on your device that help us track user preferences and improve user experience.
   - **Purpose**: To enhance the functionality of our services and personalize your experience.

5. **Image Processing**
   - **What We Collect**: NoAIWatermark processes images entirely in your browser using WebAssembly technology. Your images never leave your device and are not stored on our servers.
   - **Purpose**: To provide watermark removal functionality while maintaining complete privacy.

## Data Storage and Security

We take the security of your personal information seriously. Since NoAIWatermark processes images locally in your browser, your images never leave your device and are not stored on our servers. Any minimal data we collect (such as basic analytics) is stored securely on our servers with encryption and access controls. However, please be aware that no method of transmission over the Internet, or method of electronic storage, is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

## Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to outside parties, except in the following circumstances:

- To comply with legal obligations or respond to lawful requests from public authorities.
- To protect our rights, property, or safety, or that of our users or others.
- To provide services through trusted third-party partners who assist us in operating our website or conducting our business, subject to confidentiality agreements.

You are advised to review this privacy policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this privacy policy are effective when they are posted on this page.
